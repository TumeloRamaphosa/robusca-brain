## Narrative Arc

The distinction between AI tools and AI agents is fundamental to understanding why Studex Super Agents represent something genuinely different. A tool waits for you to use it — you have to remember to open it, prompt it, and act on what it gives you. An agent acts on your behalf, continuously, autonomously, whether you're paying attention or not. Studex built eight of them, named each after a Renaissance master, and deployed them as the operational engine running inside every company in the Studex Global Markets ecosystem.

The agents are structured as a hierarchy of intelligence and action. The Soul is the foundation — the Northstar agent that learns everything about your business: your goals, your voice, your strategy, your position in the market. Every other agent works from what Soul knows. The Obsidian Brain is the intelligence layer, continuously monitoring markets, competitors, and opportunities, feeding intelligence across the system while the company team focuses elsewhere. The Dedicated Email agent runs outreach — personalised, consistent, strategic communications going out on the company's behalf every single day without manual input.

Then there are the Compound specialists — at least five additional agents, each a master of their specific domain: content creation, partnership development, market analysis, deal structuring. Like Renaissance artists working in the same studio, each brings deep craft to their area while contributing to a shared output.

What activates the full power of the system is the five-step onboarding process. A company meets the ecosystem, maps their business with Soul (Northstar session), uploads their organisational knowledge (documents, history, processes, voice), connects their existing tools (email, CRM, social channels), and then lets the agents compound. Each action builds on the last. The pipeline grows. The presence grows. Results compound.

Pricing is structured across three tiers to match company maturity and ambition. At R3,500/month the Starter tier provides entry-level agent capability. At R10,000/month the Business tier runs all eight agents fully integrated — this is where the compounding effect fully activates. At R20,000/month the Enterprise tier delivers white-glove treatment: custom agent training, dedicated support, bespoke workflows, and full NestVM integration. All four founding partner companies operate at Enterprise tier. The critical context for any pricing conversation: in the context of what these agents replace — consultants, marketing teams, business development staff — every tier is a fraction of the cost.
